Visualising the world's billionaires

This visualisation delves into the break-down of the world's top 1%.

Wealth distribution

This map presents a vivid visual representation of the distribution of billionaires across the globe. By shading each country proportionally to its number of billionaires, patterns of wealth concentration become evident. Notably, there are striking disparities in the distribution. The USA and China, account for more than half of the billionaires. Much of the rest of the world remains lightly shaded, revealing a far lower density of such extreme wealth.

Gender Gap

It is evident that men constitute a much larger portion of global billionaires than women. It is also important to note that most people amass their wealth much later in life.

Zoom in and drag to explore the graph more closely.

Gender distribution in the top 5 countries

This graph explores in more detail the differences between countries and genders. As seen before, USA and China are well in front of the other countries.

Billionaire playground

This chart offers a vivid illustration of the industries where billionaires have amassed their fortunes. Three sectors stand out: Technology , Finance & Investments and Fashion & Retail. The dominance of the technology industry reflects our era's digital transformation. Simultaneously, the prominence of fashion represents the hold of luxury brands on the world.
